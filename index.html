About Me

My name is Matthew Sullivan and I'm an aspiring game programmer. I have a great deal of experience designing traditional board games, pen and paper RPGs and, of course, playing researching video games.

I've also studied Tai Chi, for exercise and self-defense, as well as hobby electronics. My favorite scratch-built project was a very short range FM radio transmitter. If you're ever within three feet of my home office desk, you can tune in to 103.3 FM!
